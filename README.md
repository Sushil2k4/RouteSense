# RouteSense

Guidewire DEVTrails 2026 Submission.

Intelligent system behavioural architecture that identifies and avert GPS spoofing scams in parametric insurance computer programmes.

---

##  Inspiration

The parametric weakness of the modern insurance system of gender was the situation of Market Crash - -blind trust in GPS information.

Structured money laundering plan will be able to spoof destinations in bulk hence will lead to artificial generation of disbursement and loss at the spot. This made us realize:

The geographical data is manipulated easily. Fraud is no longer a personal enterprise anymore but a professional venture. The systems must not authenticate the entries.

This motivated us to reason on a new plane of detecting fraud.



---

##  What it does

The RouteSense identifies and forestalls the fraudulent claims, although not by the means of the GPS verification, but by the means of the analysis of the multi-dimensional behavioral signals.

We are not/are not asking the question, Where is the user? but are asking:
*Does the user condition of the world make sense?

The system:

* Signs claims on environmental, behavioral and device pointers.
* Identifies the organized frauds networks using the graphical intelligence.
Dynamic ratings on trust on a claim.
* Makes sure that it does not provide fraud and imposes punishment on the actual users.

##  How we built it

Our architecture is the RouteSense architecture, a multi-engine architecture, in which the different engines have usurped another dimension of truth:

###  Reality Engine

* Makes a comparison between the information on users and weather information, traffic conditions and geographic location.
Gives non-hypocritical consistency.

Behavioral Signature Engine Behavioral signature engine is an appropriate aspect of software security assurance.

Modifies fingerprint behaviorally with:

 * Movement history
 * Claim patterns
 * Usage rhythm

###  Swarm Intelligence Engine

The assistance of graphs helps to identify clusters as a way of recognition of the fraud ring.
* Decisions made in regards to user coordination patterns.

###  Device Integrity Engine

- checks the data of GPS and sensor.
The spoofing of the device and the emulators are recognized.

###  Trust Scoring Engine

* Univariate risk measure.
* Approval, Soft Flag or Hard Flag of output decisions.

We combined:

* Rule-based validation

by models (Autoencoders)

##  What we learned

The crumbling of the systems, in regards to the fraud systems, will occur in any system that contains the single source truth (this is the case with GPS).
Signal convergence systems are the most suitable as compared to single signal detection systems.
The organisation of the fraud would need the use of graph and pattern thinking.
Security systems and user experience as well as trust and accuracy should also have a trade off.
