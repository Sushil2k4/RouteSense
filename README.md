# RouteSense

Guidewire DEVTrails 2026 Submission.

Intelligent system behavioural architecture that identifies and avert GPS spoofing scams in parametric insurance computer programmes.

---

##  Inspiration

The parametric weakness of the modern insurance system of gender was the situation of Market Crash - -blind trust in GPS information.

Structured money laundering plan will be able to spoof destinations in bulk hence will lead to artificial generation of disbursement and loss at the spot. This made us realize:

The geographical data is manipulated easily. Fraud is no longer a personal enterprise anymore but a professional venture. The systems must not authenticate the entries.

This motivated us to reason on a new plane of detecting fraud.



---

##  What it does

The RouteSense identifies and forestalls the fraudulent claims, although not by the means of the GPS verification, but by the means of the analysis of the multi-dimensional behavioral signals.

We are not/are not asking the question, Where is the user? but are asking:
*Does the user condition of the world make sense?

The system:

* Signs claims on environmental, behavioral and device pointers.
* Identifies the organized frauds networks using the graphical intelligence.
Dynamic ratings on trust on a claim.
* Makes sure that it does not provide fraud and imposes punishment on the actual users.
